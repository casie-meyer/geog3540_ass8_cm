Geovisualization Profile GEOG:3540

Multiple Choropleth Maps

This project involved investigating several variables with side-by-side choropleth maps. I created 4 choropleth maps to investigate the effect higher education has on the amount of people that have health insurance, live in mobile homes, and work in private industry. Clicking on this page leads to a site containing 4 maps. These maps can be changed by changing the data classification scheme, and the number of data classes. The maps will change according to the chosen data classification scheme and number of classes.
